I'm Taqwa Ahmed Al-Wawi, 19 years old, studying English literature at the Islamic University of Gaza. I'm a writer and poet, and through my words, I strive to amplify Gaza's voice and shed light on stories that are often left untold.

In addition to contributing to We Are Not Numbers (WANN), my work has appeared in prominent outlets including The Electronic Intifada, Mondoweiss, The Washington Report on Middle East Affairs, The Palestine Chronicle, The Markaz Review, Middle East Monitor, Al Jazeera English, Middle East Eye, The Massachusetts Review, Baladi Magazine, the Institute for Palestine Studies, Prism, the New Arab, The Intercept, Politics Today, and Truthout. My poetry has also been published by the Gaza Poets Society.
